This workshop is a hands-on introduction to: - the core Fedora 4 RESTful services - the external integration components In this session, you will work on your own laptops with a provided Vagrant image through a series of exercises that demonstrate how to use Fedora 4's core services, how to use external services, and how to think about adding new external integrations.
