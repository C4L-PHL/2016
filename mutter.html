Getting to the Mütter

A complimentary continuous shuttle service will be available to and from the Reception on Tuesday, March 8th. Shuttles will depart from the Sheraton starting at 5:30pm with the last return shuttle leaving the Mütter Museum at 8:00pm. Should you miss the last shuttle or decide to dine in the area, it is a two (2) mile walk back to the hotel through the heart of Philadelphia. There are also a number of public transport options (buses and subways).


Musical Guests: The Subfields

Joe Lucia, Dean of Temple University Libraries, has not only been instrumental in bringing Code4Lib to Philadelphia, but he is also known widely in the library community for being the guitarist of the all-librarian band, Marc Fields & Bad Data. We are honored to have the Subfields, an acoustic subset of Marc Fields & Bad Data, provide the entertainment for the evening. While this song is unlikely to make the acoustic mix, this song fully embodies the spirit of Code4Lib, and provides an example of Joe's creativity and sense of humor.
